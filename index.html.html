About Me

The marketing brain
behind the product

I spent 7 years in marketing figuring out why people respond to some products and ignore others. That question — why do some things stick and others don't — eventually pulled me into product management.

My background spans brand strategy, integrated campaigns, content, growth, and media planning across agency and client-side work. I've run campaigns for VISA, Samsung, and Novartis. I've led the full marketing function for an FMCG brand. I've watched products succeed and fail based on whether they met users where they actually were.

Now I channel that into product. I'm currently in the BeTechified PM Bootcamp, where I built out two products from scratch — Floww, a fintech platform for Nigeria's informal economy, and Calmi, a mental wellness app for users who don't yet know they need support.
